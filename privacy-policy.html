Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SRI SUPARNA MARKETING SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED ("Marketincer," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses personally identifiable information from users of our website, https://marketincer.com ("Site"), and all related services.

Since Marketincer integrates with various social networks, the information you provide or we collect may be shared with these platforms and is subject to their respective privacy policies. We do not control their data practices. To understand how your information is used by them, please review their privacy policies.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect personally identifiable information primarily to provide our services, communicate with you, and manage your registered account if you have one. If you choose not to provide the requested information, certain features may not be available to you.

Information Collected During Registration

To access restricted sections of our Site, you may need to register and provide certain personal details, including your full name, email address, and time zone. We do not store your IP address or physical location.

Use of Contact Information

We may use your contact details to send marketing communications regarding our services. If you wish to opt out of receiving such messages, you can unsubscribe using the instructions provided in the emails.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to improve website functionality and enhance user experience. Cookies do not collect personally identifiable information. Marketincer may use both session cookies (temporary) and persistent cookies (remain after closing the browser). You can modify your browser settings to manage or block cookies.

4. Information Sharing

Our primary use of your data is to publish your content on the social networks you have authenticated with. If you link your Marketincer account to a third-party social platform, your information may be shared with that service, subject to their privacy policies. For example, connecting a Google Business account involves using Google's API services, and the Google Privacy Policy will apply.

5. Access to Information

You can access and update your personal information via your account settings. To delete your data, you can remove your Marketincer account or contact us at info@marketincer.com.

6. Data Security

We use AWS servers and employ security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no security system is impenetrable. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

If you have authorized us to access your social media accounts, you can revoke access at any time. For instance, Google API users can do so via Google security settings.

7. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies before providing personal information.

8. Data Transfer

By using Marketincer, you consent to your data being processed on AWS servers, which may be located outside your country. If you reside outside these regions, your data will be transferred and stored according to applicable privacy standards.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any significant changes will be announced on our Site. It is your responsibility to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Information
